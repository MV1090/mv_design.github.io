Gravity Gun

Engine:   Unreal Engine

Platform:   Windows

Language:   Blueprints

Role:   Solo designer/ Programmer

Summary:
Gravity Gun is a puzzle game based around the common gravity gun mechanic seen in games such as half life 2.
The player must work their way through 5 different puzzles before reaching the end. These puzzles involve using the gravity gun to manipulate crates in a specific way to gain access the next puzzle.
Each puzzle use's the mechanic in a slightly different way to make a unique experience.

Highlights:

  • 5 Unique puzzles
  • Utilized Unreals physics system for the Gravity Gun mechanic
  • Drop object and Throw object mechanics
  • Camera shake when holding object
  • Visual effects for gravity beam

    • Art:
    Cave ruins pack: Created by PackDev for the FAB market place.

     Controls:
    WASD - move.
    Space - jump.
    Mouse - look around.
    Left click (no held object) - activate beam.
    Left click (held object) - launch object.
    Right click (held object) - drop object.
    R - reset objects.
