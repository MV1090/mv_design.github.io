Alice in Public Domain

Engine:   Unreal Engine

Platform:   Windows

Language:   Blueprints and C++

Role:   Development Desing Lead/ Gameplay Programmer

Summary:
Alice in public domain is a 3rd person, single player, action game, staring everyone’s favourite public domain characters.
After being dragged back in to wonderland by an evil AI, Alice must team up with her friends to free the story world of this AI threat.
As the player progresses through the game fighting back against the evil AI TV Heads, they will unlock multiple different characters, each with their own, strengths, weaknesses and unique abilities, the player will swap between the characters to maximise a combat strategy and navigate their way through the world.

Highlights:

  • Designed Multiple playable characters, with unique abilities
  • Created a unique feel between characters
  • Involved in designing smart AI enemy behaviour
  • Used a combination of C++ and blueprints to create an effective workflow
  • Set up Level streaming system to manage workflow, merge conflicts and optimization

    • Art:
    Alice in Public Domain was our capstone project; all the art comes from TFS student in the video game art program that ran in parallel to our development program.

    Gravity Gun

    Engine:   Unreal Engine

    Platform:   Windows

    Language:   Blueprints

    Role:   Solo designer/ Programmer

    Summary:
    Gravity Gun is a puzzle game based around the common gravity gun mechanic seen in games such as half life 2.
    The player must work their way through 5 different puzzles before reaching the end. These puzzles involve using the gravity gun to manipulate crates in a specific way to gain access the next puzzle.
    Each puzzle use's the mechanic in a slightly different way to make a unique experience.

    Highlights:

  • 5 Unique puzzles
  • Utilized Unreals physics system for the Gravity Gun mechanic
  • Drop object and Throw object mechanics
  • Camera shake when holding object
  • Visual effects for gravity beam

    • Art:
    Cave ruins pack: Created by PackDev for the FAB market place.

     Controls:
    WASD - move.
    Space - jump.
    Mouse - look around.
    Left click (no held object) - activate beam.
    Left click (held object) - launch object.
    Right click (held object) - drop object.
    R - reset objects.

    Paper Round

    Engine:   Unity

    Platform:   Android

    Language:   C#

    Role:   Solo designer/ Programmer

    Summary:
    Paper Round is a top-down endless runner. The player’s goal is to dodge in and out of traffic while delivering the morning newspaper.
    The player can select from 3 different characters while they play through two different game modes, in normal mode the player must deliver as many papers as they can without crashing. In timed mode, the player must deliver 100 newspapers in the quickest time without crashing.

    Highlights:

  • Global leaderboards
  • Ads
  • Object pooling for cars and houses
  • Two gamemodes with seperate leaderboards
  • Game speed increase the longer the player survives
